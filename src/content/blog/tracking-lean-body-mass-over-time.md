## Why monthly beats daily

Lean body mass changes slowly. Daily readings mostly capture water, food, and salt, which swamp the real signal. Measuring every four to six weeks gives change room to show up.

## Build a repeatable ritual

Consistency is everything. Weigh yourself under the same conditions each time:

- First thing in the morning
- After using the bathroom, before eating or drinking
- Same scale, same formula

Then run the numbers on the [lean body mass calculator](/tools/lean-body-mass-calculator) and log the result.

## What to actually watch

Track two lines over time: **lean body mass** and **body-fat percentage**. If lean mass holds or rises while body fat falls, your plan is working — even if the scale barely moves. That gap is the whole point of [tracking composition instead of weight](/guides/muscle-vs-fat-explained).

## Adjust, don't panic

One flat month isn't failure. Give any change six weeks before you judge it, then tweak one variable — usually protein or training volume. The playbook is in [how to increase lean body mass](/guides/how-to-increase-lean-body-mass).
